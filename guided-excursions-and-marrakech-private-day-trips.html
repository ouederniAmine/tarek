"MARRAKECH EXCURSION TOURS"

We organize day trips from Marrakech and Imlil.  

In Imlil, we will experience an unique mule ride that will take around 40 minutes towards the Berber village of Aroumd (Aremd) (1843m). Savor a delicious lunch which will be served in a Berber house in the village. Enjoy some free time to explore the village and take photos before you conclude your trip back in Marrakech. Day trip start on request and finishes same day in Imlil.
